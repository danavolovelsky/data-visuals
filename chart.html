1. Popularity Graph:

This graph displays the most popular genres over time, highlighting which genres have the largest audience.

2. Percentage Growth Graph:

This graph shows the genres that have grown the most in percentage terms, regardless of their overall popularity. It focuses on how much each genre's popularity has increased or decreased.

To interact with the charts:

Checkboxes:
Use the checkboxes to select which genres you want to compare and how many you want to view at once.

Zooming:
You can zoom in on specific parts of the chart by holding down and swiping close to the x-axis.
To return to the full view, simply check any of the checkboxes.
